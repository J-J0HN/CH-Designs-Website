Chloe Hicks

Mixed-Media Crafter & Ceramicist, inspired and influenced by decorative and sculptural ceramics. Creative and adept in hand building, applying decorative detail, and experimenting with glazes.

About Me.

"As a designer & maker, I am constantly inspired by the art around me, especially from nature. Whether that is the natural growths & formations of patterns, organic shapes, colours, and textures which often serve as a wellspring of ideas in my ceramics."

Services.

Freelance Design Work

Surface Pattern Designs: Creating designs for ceramic surfaces that can be transferred to tiles, tableware, or other products.

Illustration and Concept Development: Providing illustrations and concept designs for ceramics-related projects or publications.

Custom Ceramic Commissions

Bespoke Pottery & Tableware: Creating unique, custom-designed pottery, such as pots, mugs, and coasters, tailored to client specifications.

Interior Decor Pieces: Designing and crafting ceramic vases, wall art, and decorative items for interior spaces.

Restoration and Repair Services

Ceramic Restoration: Repairing and restoring broken or damaged ceramic items, including antiques or cherished heirlooms.

Glaze Matching and Refinishing: Matching original glazes and refinishing ceramics to restore them to their former condition.

Collaborative Projects

Architectural Ceramics: Collaborating with architects and interior designers to create custom ceramic tiles, murals, or installations for buildings.

Fashion and Jewelry: Designing and producing ceramic/resin jewelry or accessories in collaboration with fashion designers.

Interdisciplinary Art Projects: Working with artists from other disciplines (e.g., metalwork, glass) to create mixed-media artworks.

Art Exhibitions and Curation

Curatorial Services: Curating exhibitions that focus on ceramics, helping to select, organize, and present works.

Workshops and Teaching

Ceramics Workshops: Offering classes or workshops to teach various ceramic techniques, such as hand-building or glazing.

Online Courses: Creating and selling online courses focused on specific ceramic skills or projects.

Educational Collaborations: Partnering with schools, community centers, or art studios to provide ceramic education.

Consultation and Advisory Services

Material and Glaze Consultation: Advising other artists or businesses on the best ceramic materials, glazes, and firing techniques for their projects.

Studio Setup Consultation: Assisting individuals or organizations in setting up their own ceramics studio, including equipment recommendations and layout planning.
